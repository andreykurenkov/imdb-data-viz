This chart visualizes the number of movies genre releases from 1915 to 2014, as either their absolute counts or percentage of releases. The data is gotten from IMDB with this code, and then further post-processed with D3 and visualized with dimple.js. These are total counts of genre tags rather than movies, since a single movie may tagged as both a Comedy and Romance, and some filtering is done to avoid counting incredibely obscure movies by not counting movies with less than 10 IMDB user votes. The visualization conveys an intentionally broad story about the relative popularity of genres and movies overall over the last 90 years, with various interesting patterns such as the rise of the documentary starting from the 60s and the decrease of the popularity of action movies past the 80s.
